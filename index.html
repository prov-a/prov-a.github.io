PROV-A, the Provenance App

Welcome to PROV-A

Provenance traces the journey of artefacts—from creation to their present location in galleries, libraries, archives, and museums (GLAM).
PROV-A bridges the gap between traditional provenance studies and modern digital methods. By integrating expert human curation within a semi-automated workflow, PROV-A empowers art historians to transform free-text provenance records into Linked Open Data (LOD). This approach supports new opportunities for analysis, collaboration, and discovery, harnessing both human expertise and technology.
